Ahead of you, you'll see that the ground has a brownish tint to it. This hints you about the presence of an exit zone. Walking into an exit zone will teleport you somewhere else..

There are two types of exit zones :
Local exit zones will take you to another level, or to another place in the same level.
Worldmap exit zones will take you back to the worldmap.

Move forward into the brownish tiles to go to the Worldmap.

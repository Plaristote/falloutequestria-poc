Combat mode has been enabled !

When enemies have sighted you, or when you take aim with one of your attacks, combat mode gets enabled. Once your turn come, any action you take will cost a given amount of action points. Your available action points are represented by the dots on the top of the HUD:

Attacking a rat:
You do not have a weapon right now, so we will first learn how to kill a rat using your unarmed attacks. Start by moving in contact to the nearest rat:

Then, select one of your two quick actions, in the middle of the HUD:

You should now be in target mode. Aim at the rat you want to hit, and click it:

The percentage showing next to the target cursor hints at how likely it is that you'll manage to successfully hit the target.

Ending your turn
If you are out of actions to take, but still have some remaining action points, you can also pass your turn by clicking the Pass turn button.

Ending combat
If there are no more enemies around you, clicking Pass turn will end combat. You cannot leave combat unless your enemies are all dead, or have lost track of you.

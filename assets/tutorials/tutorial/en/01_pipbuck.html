Welcome to the tutorial !

We'll start by taking a look at the PipBuck ! You can open the PipBuck by clicking the PIP button at the bottom right side of the screen, or by pressing the P key.

The PipBuck features several interfaces:
Clock Allow you to rest and sleep.
Quests Records all data on your ongoing or completed goals.
Archives Lists all the files downloaded on your PipBuck.
Automap Map of your surroundings.

Your first quest has already begun ! Open your PipBuck, and click on the "Tutorial" quest.

What to do next ?
Click on the "next" button on the top left corner of the screen to move on to the next tutorial page.

Congratulations ! You won your first combat !

We've rewarded you with additional experience, to show you how leveling up works.

Leveling up grants you skill points, and occasionally perks.

Skill points:
You can use skill points to improve your skills, making it more likely for you to succeed when performing various type of actions: lockpicking a door, using your unarmed, melee or ranged attacks, etc.
Some skills will also open up new dialog options with some NPCs (this is mostly true for the Speech skill).

Perks:
Perks give your character specific bonuses. They are gained every few levels (depending on your race and traits), and more of them will become available as your character's skills improve.

Character sheet:
To assign your skill points or perks, you'll need to open your character sheet On the HUD, clik on the CHAR button, left of the quick use buttons.

When perks are available, a pop-up will appear to let you pick one.
Skills are displayed on the right-hand of the interface: click on the +/- buttons to assign your available skill points. Skills you are proficient in (highlighted in yellow) will improve twice as fast as your other skills.

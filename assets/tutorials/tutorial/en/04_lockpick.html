The door is locked.

There are always several ways to solve an issue. When confronted to a locked door, you could look for corresponding key, or try to blast it open with explosives, or try to pick the lock.

Open your skilldex using the S key, and select the lockpick skill.

You should now be in target mode, and the cursor should look like this:

While in target mode, click on the door to trigger the action you picked beforehand. Your character will then try to reach the door, and attempt to lockpick it.

If you do not succeed on first try, you can always try again. But beware : if you end up breaking the lock, the only solution left will be to blast the door.
